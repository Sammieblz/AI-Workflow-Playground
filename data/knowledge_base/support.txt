Customer Support Information:

Technical Support:
- We provide technical support for all our products
- Support hours: Monday-Friday 9 AM - 5 PM
- Response time: Within 24 hours for email, immediate for phone calls
- Support channels: Email, phone, and online chat

Common Issues and Solutions:

1. Login Problems:
   - Reset password using the "Forgot Password" link
   - Check if caps lock is enabled
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Contact support if issues persist

2. Payment Issues:
   - Verify billing information is correct
   - Check with your bank for any holds
   - Try a different payment method
   - Contact billing support for assistance

3. Product Questions:
   - Check our FAQ section first
   - Review product documentation
   - Contact product support for specific questions
   - Schedule a demo if needed

4. Account Management:
   - Update profile information in account settings
   - Change password regularly for security
   - Review subscription details
   - Contact support for account changes
